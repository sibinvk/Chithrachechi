Krishnan Nair Shantakumari Chithra, known as K.S. Chithra or Chitra, is an Indian playback singer and Carnatic musician. Fondly called "Chinna Kuyil" (Little Nightingale), she has recorded over 25,000 songs in 16 different languages including Malayalam, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Hindi, Bengali, Oriya, Punjabi, Badaga, Urdu, Sanskrit, Tulu, Rajasthani, and Assamese.

Born on July 27, 1963, in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala, Chithra is a recipient of 6 National Film Awards, 36 different state film awards, and numerous other accolades. She was honored with the Padma Bhushan in 2006, India's third-highest civilian honor.
